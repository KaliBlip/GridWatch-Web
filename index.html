Built for the field

Everything a grid operator checks, on one screen

GridWatch mirrors the readouts your team already trusts — no new instruments to learn, just faster access to the ones you have.

Live metrics per node

Voltage, current, power load, temperature, and frequency, refreshed automatically as new readings come in from each transformer.

List or map, your call

Scan every node as a list, or switch to a map of Ghana with color-coded pins. Filter by region, status, or device type.

Alerts the moment they happen

Overheating, overload, low voltage, or a node going dark — push notifications reach you before the fault does any damage.

A history for every node

Trend charts over 24 hours, 7 days, or 30 days, plus a full alert log, so you can tell a spike from a pattern.

Find it, then find the site

Every node carries its exact GPS pin, with one tap through to directions when someone needs to be there in person.

Report and schedule, in place

Log an issue or schedule maintenance right from a node's detail screen, so nothing gets tracked in a separate app.

A dark, high-contrast interface built to be read at a glance — with a light theme available for daytime fieldwork.
