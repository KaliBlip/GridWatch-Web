Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

GridWatch is built for field engineers and grid operators who monitor transformer infrastructure. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how it's handled — in plain terms, without the legal filler.

1. What we collect

To provision your account and run the app, we collect:

  • Account information — name, work email, organization, and role, provided when your administrator sets up your access or when you sign in through your organization's SSO.
  • Device information — device type, operating system version, and a push notification token, used to deliver alerts to the right device.
  • App activity — which nodes you view, alerts you acknowledge, and issues or maintenance requests you file, so your team has a shared record of who did what.

GridWatch does not collect your personal location. Location data in the app refers to the fixed GPS coordinates of transformer installations, not to the device running the app.

2. Transformer node data

GridWatch displays operational data streamed from IoT sensors installed on transformer equipment, structured as channel fields (voltage, current, power load, temperature, and related readings) and relayed through your organization's ThingSpeak backend. This data describes equipment, not people, and is owned and controlled by the utility or organization operating the transformer network.

3. How we use it

We use the information above to:

  • Authenticate you and apply the correct permissions for your organization
  • Display live and historical node data relevant to your account
  • Send push notifications for alerts you've opted into
  • Maintain an audit trail of issue reports and maintenance schedules
  • Diagnose technical problems and improve app reliability

4. Sharing and third parties

We don't sell personal data. Information is shared only in these cases:

  • Your organization — administrators within your organization can see account and activity data for members of their team.
  • ThingSpeak / IoT backend — node metrics are read from the channel your organization has configured; we don't route this data to any additional third party.
  • Service providers — infrastructure providers (such as hosting and push notification services) that process data strictly on our behalf, under agreements limiting their use of it.
  • Legal requirements — if required to comply with applicable law or a valid legal request.

5. Data retention

Account data is retained for as long as your organization keeps your account active. Historical node metrics are retained according to your organization's ThingSpeak channel settings. Alert and maintenance logs are retained for 24 months to support audit and reporting needs, unless your organization requests earlier deletion.

6. Security

Data in transit is encrypted, and access to node data and account information is restricted by role. API keys and ThingSpeak channel credentials are visible only to administrators within your organization's account settings.

7. Your rights

You can request a copy of your account data, ask us to correct inaccurate information, or request deletion of your account, by contacting us or your organization's administrator. Because node data belongs to your organization, requests to modify or remove operational data should go through your administrator.

8. Children's privacy

GridWatch is a professional tool for field engineers and grid operators. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page and notify administrators through the app or by email.

10. Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to privacy@gridwatch.app, or through our support page.
